About Us

Cyber Sages is a dedicated robotics team in our third year of the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). Comprised of talented freshman, sophomore, junior, and senior students, we are driven by a passion for innovation and technology. Our diverse skills and collaborative spirit propel us to achieve excellence in robotics, fostering creativity and technical expertise.
