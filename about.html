About Us

Cyber Sages is a dedicated robotics team in our second year of the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). Comprised of talented sophomore and junior students, we are driven by a passion for innovation and technology. Our diverse skills and collaborative spirit propel us to achieve excellence in robotics, fostering creativity and technical proficiency.

Past Achievements

2023-2024 Season

Competed in the FTC Regional Qualifiers, earning a top 10 finish and the Rookie Team Award for outstanding performance.

2022-2023 Season

Founded Cyber Sages, debuted in our first FTC season, and engineered our initial robot, 'SageBot,' with a focus on teamwork and innovation.
