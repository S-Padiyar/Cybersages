What is FIRST?

FIRST is a renowned non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring students in STEM fields through exciting robotics competitions. Their programs include FIRST LEGO League, FIRST Tech Challenge, and FIRST Robotics Competition, each catering to different age groups and offering unique challenges that encourage teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

About FTC

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an exciting robotics competition for middle and high school students. Teams design, build, and program robots to tackle diverse game challenges, fostering innovation and teamwork in an exhilarating environment that inspires young minds in STEM fields.
