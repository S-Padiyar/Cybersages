About Us

We are a dynamic and enthusiastic robotics team, taking part in our second year in the world of FTC (First Tech Challenge). In our next year, we are proud to introduce ourselves as a dedicated group of sophomore and junior students, who are deeply passionate about robotics and technology. With a shared vision of innovation, we’ve embarked on this exciting journey to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the realm of robotics. Our diverse backgrounds and drive to learn, form our team’s foundation. We are committed to learning, and collaborating as a team, as we start this new path in FTC.
